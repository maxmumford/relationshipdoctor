Relationship Doctor is a relationship therapy and marriage counselling service run by Peter Mumford at Effective Counselling. It is based in Shoreham-by-Sea, West Sussex, England, and with over 40 years experience, provides a unique approach to relationship therapy that can help you find love again!

Please feel free to get in touch if you would like to chat with us about how we can help.
